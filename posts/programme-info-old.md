The SGUS Programmes offered by Singapore Media Academy are designed to provide jobseekers with training to boost their employability and competitive edge. These funded full-time training programmes range in 4 different disciplines in Media, Digital/Social Media Marketing, and Online Content Creation. The courses are eligible for Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents aged 21 and above.Participants must be able to commit to the training programme on a full-time basis.Each participant will receive a training allowance of S$1,200.00 per month, throughout the course of study.Â The course fees are highly subsidised, ranging from a full nett fee of S$500 to S$1,000 for each programme, and the nett course fees are SkillsFuture Credits eligible.All courses are full-time programmes of duration 9 to 11 months each, comprising blended learning - virtual classes and face-2-face training.The courses are delivered by Industry Professionals.For more information on the SGUS Programmes, the Eligibility Requirements and the Frequently Asked Questions, click here.

SGUS - Writers' Training Programme
SSG Course Ref No.: SGUS-PS-SMA-001 | Funding Valid Till 31 December 2020
A full-time training programme in writing that covers most forms of writing from narrative scripts and documentaries for film, TV, and broadcast to copywriting and the editing of copywriting to business pitches and proposals. The programme also trains attendees in team management skills, problem-solving skills, and fundamentals in managing IP for copyright materials.
Course Content:

Fundamentals Of A Professional In A Digital Workplace
Getting Headhunted Online
Write A Narrative Script
Manage Communications In A Creative Environment
Write A Documentary Script
Breakdown A Script
Write A Sitcom Script
Write Copy For A Range Of Contexts
Administer And Manage The Use Of Licensed Copyright Materials Created
Address Problems Which Impact On The Work
Write And Edit Copy For Interactive Media Projects
Write A Concept Proposal To Pitch To A Client
Course Date: 5 October 2020 to 30 June 2021

SGUS - Production Crew Training Programme
SSG Course Ref No.: SGUS-ICM-SMA-002 | Funding Valid Till 31 December 2020
A full-time training programme to impart the skills and know-how of being an effective member of a production crew. The programme covers pre-production, analysing production needs from a script to lighting requirements, image framing, and image quality to mounting lights and audio requirements of a production.

Install Lighting Equipment, Accessories And Mounting Equipment
Set Up Lights To Achieve The Desired Technical And Aesthetic Requirements Of A Shoot
Manage Audio Equipment For A Recording Session
Control Image Quality During Shooting
Devise Camera Angles And Coverage With The Director Of Photography / Cameraman And Director To Maximise Dramatic Effect
Compose, Frame And Track Image
Course Date: 5 October 2020 to 31 August 2021
