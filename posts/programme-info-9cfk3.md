Mode of Training

Assessment

Some modules will conclude with a compulsory assessment component.
Assessments may include role plays, quizzes, assignments, projects, and written examinations.
Participants need to achieve at least 75% attendance for each module before they are allowed to sit for the assessment.

Certification
Participants will receive a Certificate of Attendance issued by Singapore Media Academy for each module completed.
Completion of all the required modules will qualify candidates for the Certificate of Completion in SGUS - Social Media Marketing Training Programme.
