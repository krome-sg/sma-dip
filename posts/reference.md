Our part-time diplomas are crafted to empower you with practical knowledge and expertise.Date, Time & Venue: Saturday, 24 August 2024, 11:30am to 6pm, at 1 Stars Avenue, Mediacorp Campus, Singapore 138507Course Preview Sessions & Guest Speakers:
11:30am to 1:45pm: WSQ Diploma in Media (Content Production and Management) - Assistant Director, and WSQ Diploma in Media (Content Production and Management) - Scriptwriter(This session also includes the SkillsFuture Funding Information Talk from 1pm to 1:45pm.)
David Suen has been a working director in the local media scene for over 20 years. Being a freelancer, he has garnered extensive experience in many broadcast genres, helping numerous production houses direct their TV programmes locally and internationally with over 50 dramas and documentaries, 15 commercials, 66 corporate & event videos, and counting.
Janet Liau started life in broadcast as a researcher writer for variety and info-ed programmes. She then went to work in trade development, business consulting, advertising, and media sales before returning to broadcast in 2009. Her varied work experience helped her to become a better storyteller.
11:30am to 1:45pm: WSQ Diploma in Media (Post-Production) - Sound Editor/Sound Engineer(This session also includes the SkillsFuture Funding Information Talk from 1pm to 1:45pm.)
Being both right- and left-brained, Fritz Lim is well-versed in sound design and mixing, Python and C# programming, mobile app development, and web development for kids, teens, and adults.
1pm to 4pm: Specialist Diploma in Social Media Marketing and Online Content Creation(This session also includes the SkillsFuture Funding Information Talk from 1pm to 1:45pm.)
Ray Pang is an award-winning filmmaker, both locally and internationally. Ray was also nominated as the finalist for Yahoo Singapore 9 and runner-up for the Entertainment Category. During the same year, his short film ‘Closer To Me’ was shortlisted for the prestigious Louis Vuitton's second edition Journey Awards, making him the only Singaporean nominated for the Jury's awards People's Choice Award of the contest. The film later gets to participate in the 66th Festival de Cannes's Short Film Corner.
1pm to 4pm: Diploma in Media (Post-Production) - Video Editor(This session also includes the SkillsFuture Funding Information Talk from 1pm to 1:45pm.)
Noraswan Mohamed has more than 15 years of experience in the creative and media industry. He is highly proficient in current motion graphic design skills and practices and has an excellent portfolio of up-to-date commercial design work.
4:30pm to 6pm: WSQ Specialist Diploma in Media (Content Production and Management) - Producer
Juan Foo's film journey began in script reading and distribution for a film finance company, then freelancing in editing and production. Juan was one of the first Asian filmmakers to workshop projects in European labs like Produire-Au-Sud. He was the associate producer for other indie-Singapore films such as ‘Road Less Travelled’ (1997), ‘Talking Cock The Movie’ (2002), and ‘Unarmed Combat’ (2005). He developed the bubble tea comedy ‘One Thousand Cups to Midnight’ (2020). During the COVID-19 pandemic, Juan produced Singapore's first creature monster film, ‘Circle Line’ (2023).
4:30pm to 6pm: Diploma in Acting (Chinese) 演员训练班 (专科文凭)
Liow Shi Suen has worked in the media industry for over 30 years, with experience spanning from voice-recording director to variety programme producer, emcee, and dubbing artist. Her passion for acting is also shown through her involvement in stage acting, where she took on numerous roles.
