Course Modules

Plan the Production Budget, Schedule and Methods of Delivery
Plan and Manage Locations
Coordinates Film or Television Camera Requirements on a Production Shoot
Assist the Director in the Co-ordination of all Production Activity, and Supervise the Cast and Crew
Set Up Lights to Achieve the Desired Technical and Aesthetic Requirements of a Shoot
Devise Camera Angles and Coverage with the Director of Photography / Cameraman and Director to Maximise Dramatic Effect
Manage Audio Equipment for a Recording Session
Manage Post-Production Process to Ensure a Coherent and Integrated Production

Mode of Training
Theory & Practical
Assessment

All modules will conclude with a compulsory assessment component.
Assessments may include role plays, quizzes, assignments, projects, and written examinations.
Participants need to achieve at least 75% attendance for each module before they are allowed to sit for the assessment.

Certification
Participants will receive a WSQ Statement of Attainment (SOA) upon successful completion of each module and its assessment.
Completion of all the required modules will qualify candidates for the WSQ Diploma in Film & Television (Production) under the Creative Industries Workforce Skills Qualification (CI WSQ) framework.
Participants will also receive a Certificate of Attendance issued by Singapore Media Academy for each module completed.
