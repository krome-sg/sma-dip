IMPORTANT NOTICE: SCAM AND PHISHING ALERT
          
We are aware of various unauthorised parties falsely claiming to be affiliated with Singapore Media Academy (SMA). Such parties may use various methods of communication to impersonate SMA, including via websites, phone, social media, or messaging platforms. If you receive unsolicited messages claiming to be from SMA, please exercise caution. When in doubt, always verify any communications by writing to us directly via marketing@MediaAcademy.sg.Mediacorp and Singapore Media Academy do not use any messaging platforms for recruitment. All job openings are posted on our Careers page: mediacorp.sg/careers.Online Links Policy - Mediacorp
