Specialist Diploma in Social Media Marketing and Online Content CreationOnline Preview Session: 12 February 2025, Wednesday, 7:30pm to 9:30pm, via ZoomGuest Speaker: Ray Pang, Award-winning Filmmaker
Ray was nominated as the finalist for Yahoo Singapore 9 and runner-up for the Entertainment Category. During the same year, his short film ‘Closer To Me’ was shortlisted for the prestigious Louis Vuitton's second edition Journey Awards, making him the only Singaporean nominated for the Jury's awards People's Choice Award of the contest. The film later gets to participate in the 66th Festival de Cannes's Short Film Corner.
WSQ Specialist Diploma in Media (Content Production and Management) - ProducerOnline Preview Session: 19 February 2025, Wednesday, 7:30pm to 9:30pm, via ZoomGuest Speaker: Juan Foo, Pioneering Independent Film Producer
Juan’s film journey began in script reading and distribution for a film finance company, then freelancing in editing and production. Juan was one of the first Asian filmmakers to workshop projects in European labs like Produire-Au-Sud. He was the associate producer for other indie-Singapore films such as ‘Road Less Travelled’ (1997), ‘Talking Cock The Movie’ (2002), and ‘Unarmed Combat’ (2005). He developed the bubble tea comedy ‘One Thousand Cups to Midnight’ (2020). During the COVID-19 pandemic, Juan produced Singapore's first creature monster film, ‘Circle Line’ (2023).ProgrammesFeatured
