Full-Time Study | Course Fee SubsidySingapore's e-commerce market is forecast to rise to US$10 Billion by 2026*. One of the key ingredients in the success of an e-commerce business is creating compelling and impactful content to attract, engage & convert your online audience. Content Marketing has evolved tremendously in recent years, with the rise of new social media platforms, live-streaming, and short-form videos. These new trends are set to fuel significant future growth.With this, Singapore Media Academy (CET in Media, Broadcast and Film) and Singapore Institute of Retail Studies (CET in Retail) present the Professional Certificate in Digital Retail Entertainment, a course designed to equip marketers with the skills to develop an effective content marketing strategy and the know-how to create engaging video content.

This course is suitable for:Marketing Professionals looking to gain an edge in their career development in the retail entertainment industryCareer switchers who are keen to enter the retail-entertainment industry and have no prior relevant backgroundMalls/ Business owners who are keen to venture into e-commerce with live broadcastProfessional Bloggers, artiste, etc who want to generate commercial revenue via Live Broadcast

Course Modules
1. Alibaba Content Marketing Programme (Conducted in English/Mandarin) 
Conducted by Singapore Institute of Retail Studies (SIRS)
The module is designed for participants to learn the most efficient way to relate a brand story to target audiences, keeping them interested and engaged with meaningful videos that drive the right messages through different platforms. The knowledge and technical skills imparted will enable participants to connect with their online community, generate leads/customers, create brand awareness, establish a positive brand reputation and build customers’ trust and loyalty.
Outline:

Optimise Content Marketing Strategy and Master Short Video Creation Skills 
Successful Live-streaming Operations 
Scale up on your Social Media Platform Operations & grow your Fans Economy

2. Making Online Content (Conducted in English)
Conducted by Singapore Media Academy (SMA)
This module will cover the basics in story structure, what makes a compelling story and how it can and has been applied to different mediums like film, television and stage plays. Covering the A to Z of making good and compelling content and/ or short videos from concept, pre-production and production to post-production using entry-level equipment and software like mobile phones and complimentary editing software.
Learning Outcome:

Understand story structure, plot design and variants of the three-act structure
Develop storylines and plot structure for online content
Write basic narrative scripts for online content
Learn pre-production process for making online content
Make content for online purposes
Edit and finish online content

Mode of Training
In-Person and/or Online
Assessment
Each module comes with specific individual and/or group assessments, which participants are required to complete successfully. Participants will need to be marked Competent for all the modules to be awarded the Professional Certificate in Digital Retail Entertainment.

The assessments can take the form of written quizzes, short questions and answers or group work presentations. Lecturers will advise accordingly in class. Participants will also need to complete a final group project presentation at the end of the programme.
Certification
Participants certified Competent for all the modules will be awarded the Professional Certificate in Digital Retail Entertainment.
