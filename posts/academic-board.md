The Academic Board is responsible for the academic quality of the Singapore Media Academy.  It oversees all matters relating to admission, course content, criteria, and quality of lecturers’ appointment and performance. The member will serve a term of two (2) years from the date of appointment.Academic Board Members will meet at least twice a year for any of the duties as follows:Advise the types of courses to be offeredEndorse new certificate programmesDevelop and review policies and procedures on Private Education Institution (PEI) academic mattersFacilitate the implementation and compliance with such policies and proceduresDevelop a set of standards to ensure the course review, including, but not limited to:The content of the modules or subjects of each courseThe duration of the course; andThe appropriate entry and graduation requirementsApprove each trainer to be deployed to teach any course/module/subject offered by SMA after determining the trainer possesses the minimum qualifications and experienceThe Academic Board consists of the following members:Mr Man Shu Sum, Veteran Media Industry ProfessionalDr Yek Tiew Ming, Principal, ITE College EastMr Timothy Tan Choon Hwee, Head, Singapore Media Academy
