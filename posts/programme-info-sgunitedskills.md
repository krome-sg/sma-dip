The SGUS Programmes offered by Singapore Media Academy are designed to provide jobseekers with training to boost their employability and competitive edge. These funded full-time training programmes range in 4 different disciplines in Media, Digital/Social Media Marketing, and Online Content Creation. The courses are eligible for Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents aged 21 and above.Participants must be able to commit to the training programme on a full-time basis.Each participant will receive a training allowance of S$1,200.00 per month, throughout the course of study.Â The course fees are highly subsidised, ranging from a full nett fee of S$500 to S$1,000 for each programme, and the nett course fees are SkillsFuture Credits eligible.All courses are full-time programmes of duration 9 to 11 months each, comprising blended learning - virtual classes and face-2-face training.The courses are delivered by Industry Professionals.For more information on the SGUS Programmes, the Eligibility Requirements and the Frequently Asked Questions, click here.
